How to use

For Quran

    • Click on the Quran section at the main page of DeeniFriend you'll be greeted with a list of Qurans, click on the "The Quran" it'll take you the Quran Reader
      After it is launced then click on the visible play button then the Quran will be loaded within few seconds
      You can directly start reading, or if you want to seek to other Surah or Juz you can click on "Content" button at the top left extreme and it'll show you the list of Surah or Juz you can click on any of it and you'll be taken there directly
      If you want to save the progress of your reading you can click the "Bookmark" button at the top left extreme beside contents button and save your page progress
      You can also change the font Size from the "Settings" button located at top right extreme, and can adjust the font as per your needs

    • For Hadith

    • You can follow the same instructions as mentioned above for Hadith as well, for the Hadith books you need to click the "Hadith" section on the main page and three main Hadith books are listed there respectively
      Click on anyone of it and start reading

    • Quran Audio Player

    For Quran listening, you need to go to "Quran" section then click on "Quran Listening", a webpage with the player will be loaded and by default it'll be at Juz 1 you can play next Juz by clicking on "next" button and can seek throughout the Juz and also adjust the volume

    Quran English Summary

    • For listening the brief Quran Summary by Juz. Go to Quran section and click on "Quran Summary-English"
      A youtube playlist will be loaded with the Juz 1 summary episode
      And you can change the Juz by clicking the playlist button at the top right extreme positon and then clicking the Juz of your choice, and it'll load consequently

    • And that is all of it. Enjoy learning insha'Allah
